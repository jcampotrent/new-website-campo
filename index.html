Project 1 - Milestone Power Supply

The project involves building and programming a power supply by soldering components onto a PCB. First, gather components like resistors, capacitors, diodes, and a voltage regulator. Next, solder the components onto the PCB according to the schematic, ensuring correct placement and avoiding short circuits. Once assembled, program the power supply to meet the desired voltage and current specifications. Finally, test the power supply with a multimeter to verify output and troubleshoot any issues to ensure it functions properly.
